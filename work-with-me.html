Work With Me

Chris K. N.

If you want to work with me, you have a few options.

First off, while I do a lot of writing both for myself and clients, I rarely take pure copywriting or journalism jobs anymore. You should feel free to pitch me, but it will have to be something special.

However, as head of Papaya design & marketing's New York office, I work with a great team of designers, writers, marketers, coders and others. If you're in need of solid marketing strategies and great advertsing, we should get together.

Hiring an ad agency – even one as easy-going as Papaya – is not for everyone, though. However, I also really enjoy helping creatives and entrepreneurs with one-on-one consulting and coaching.

If any of this sounds interesting, don't hesistate to get in touch. Use the contact form below, or some other method found on the Contact page.
